Lagos Traffic Tips

Advice to help you survive the roads every day.

⏰

Leave Early

Lagos traffic peaks between 7am–9am and 4pm–7pm. Leaving 30 minutes earlier can save you over an hour.

🛣️

Use Alternative Routes

The Third Mainland Bridge is not always the fastest. Routes through Ikoyi or Carter Bridge are often quicker.

📻

Listen to Traffic Reports

Radio stations like Smooth FM and Lagos Traffic Radio give live road updates during peak hours.

⛽

Keep Your Tank Full

Lagos traffic can be unpredictable. Never leave home with a near-empty tank — you might be stuck for hours.

📱

Report What You See

If you spot an accident or road block, report it on SmartRoute so other drivers can avoid that route.

🔖

Save Your Regular Routes

Save your daily routes so you can check traffic status quickly every morning before leaving.
